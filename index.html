At MyPrayerPal, we understand the sacred nature of your spiritual journey and are committed to protecting your privacy with the highest standards of Islamic ethics and modern data protection principles.

Information We Collect

MyPrayerPal may collect the following types of information to enhance your Islamic prayer experience:

  • Rakaat Counter with AI: Our app uses your device camera with AI and Machine Learning to count rakaats during prayer. Important: The camera does not record, store, or transmit any video data - it only processes movement in real-time for counting purposes
  • Location Information: Your location to provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction (only if you grant permission)
  • Address Information: With your explicit permission, we may store your home address for more precise prayer time calculations
  • Messages: Communications and messages you send through the app for support or feedback purposes
  • Device Information: Basic device details for technical support and app optimization

How We Use Your Information

Following Islamic principles of trust (Amanah), we use your information solely to:

  • Provide accurate prayer times based on your location
  • Calculate precise Qibla direction for proper prayer orientation
  • Count rakaats during prayer using AI analysis of your movements (no video recording)
  • Store your address (with permission) for enhanced prayer time accuracy
  • Process and respond to your messages and support requests
  • Improve our app's Islamic features and AI accuracy

Camera and AI Technology

Rakaat Counter Feature: Our innovative AI-powered rakaat counter uses your device camera to help count your prayer movements. Here's what you need to know:

  • The camera analyzes your movements in real-time only
  • No video recording takes place at any time
  • No visual data is stored on your device or our servers
  • The AI processes movement patterns locally on your device
  • You maintain complete control and can disable this feature anytime
  • Camera access is requested only when you use the rakaat counter
